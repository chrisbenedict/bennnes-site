Perfect Pasta Techniques

June 15, 2025

After years of cooking pasta, I've finally mastered the art of achieving that perfect al dente texture. The secret isn't just in the timing—it's in understanding your pasta, your water, and your sauce.

Here are my top tips:

  • Use plenty of salted water (like sea water!)
  • Save that starchy pasta water
  • Finish cooking the pasta in the sauce
  • Never rinse your pasta (unless making a cold salad)
